Magnetic Field
$$ \dot{x}_1 = \cos(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = \sin(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 \text{ remains unchanged} $$
Double Pendulum
$$ \dot{v}_1 = \frac{\sin(4\pi(x_1-x_2))}{2(\cos(2\pi(x_1-x_2))-2)}v_1^2 - \frac{\sin(2\pi(x_1-x_2))}{2-\cos(2\pi(x_1-x_2))}v_2^2 $$
$$ \dot{v}_2 = - \frac{2 \sin(2\pi(x_1-x_2))}{2-\cos(2\pi(x_1-x_2))} v_1^2 - \frac{\sin(4\pi(x_1-x_2))}{2(\cos(2\pi(x_1-x_2))-2)} v_2^2 $$
Example 1: Magnetic Field: zero
$$ \dot{x}_1 = \cos(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = \sin(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 = 0 $$
Example 2: Magnetic Field: constant
$$ \dot{x}_1 = \cos(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = \sin(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 = 1 $$
Example 3: Magnetic Field: Taimanov
$$ \dot{x}_1 = \cos(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = \sin(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 = \cos(2 \pi x_1) $$
Example 4: Magnetic Field: tunnel
$$ \dot{x}_1 = \cos(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = \sin(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 = \sin(2 \pi x_1) + \cos(2 \pi x_2) $$
Example 5: default
$$ \dot{x}_1 = 2 \pi \sin(2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = \sin(2 \pi x_2) + 2 \cos (2 \pi x_3) $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 = \cos(2 \pi x_1) $$
Example 6: 3-sphere
$$ \dot{x}_1 = v_1 $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = v_2 $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 = v_3 $$
$$ \dot{x}_4 = v_4 $$
$$ \dot{v}_1 = -x_1 $$
$$ \dot{v}_2 = -x_2 $$
$$ \dot{v}_3 = -x_3 $$
$$ \dot{v}_4 = -x_4 $$
Example 7: Magnetic 3-sphere
$$ \dot{x}_1 = v_1 $$
$$ \dot{x}_2 = v_2 $$
$$ \dot{x}_3 = v_3 $$
$$ \dot{x}_4 = v_4 $$
$$ \dot{v}_1 = -s v_2-(1-s \delta) x_1 $$
$$ \dot{v}_2 = s v_1-(1-s \delta) x_2 $$
$$ \dot{v}_3 = -s v_4-(1-s \delta) x_3 $$
$$ \dot{v}_4 = s v_3-(1-s \delta) x_4 $$
$$ s = 0.5 $$

Tree View Controls

Click on a node in the tree view to select what is shown in the properties view:

  • left click without modifiers: deselect all nodes and select clicked node and all childen (recursively)

The following modifiers change the behavior:

  • ctrl: add/remove from selection
  • shift: include children

The modifiers can be combined.
